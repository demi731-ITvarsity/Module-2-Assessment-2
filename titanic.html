Age Restriction:PG13


Synopsis of the movie:Titanic is a movie about a seventeen-year-old aristocrat named Rose who falls in love with a kind but poor artist named Jack aboard the luxurious, ill-fated R.M.S. Titanic1. The narrative unfolds in a dual timeline, with an elderly Rose narrating her experiences to treasure hunter Brock Lovett, who is searching for a valuable necklace called the Heart of the Ocean on the wreck of the Titanic in the present day2. The movie is directed by James Cameron and stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet1.
