Synopsis of the movie:“We Grown Now” is a poignant coming-of-age film set in 1992 Chicago, directed by Minhal Baig. The story revolves around two best friends, Malik and Eric, who navigate the joys and hardships of growing up in the Cabrini-Green public housing complex.


Showing Times:Monday & Sunday 13:00pm to 17:00pm
